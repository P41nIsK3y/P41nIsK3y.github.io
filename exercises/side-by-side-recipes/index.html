Tiramisu French Toast With Coffee Anglaise

Prep time: 25 Minutes
Cook Time: 20 Minutes
Total Time: 45 Minutes
Yields: 6

When you're craving dessert for breakfast, make this tiramisu French toast. A silky coffee anglaise is poured all around, soaking into every slice, then it's topped with the most delicious whipped mascarpone white chocolate cream and a dusting of cocoa powder. All the components of tiramisu, just reimagined in brunch form.
